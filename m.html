Studio 20:
Digital First

Journalism is changing. We know that. Journalism school has to change with it. Welcome to Studio 20. We train people for what journalism is becoming— not for where it’s been.

First, we teach you to master the digital landscape, and introduce you to journalists in New York who are making the change happen. Then you work on projects with media companies that have to innovate to survive. To graduate you design your own project, and we help you find a partner for it. Along the way you acquire new skills.

This not a drill. You have to deliver. Our projects are 100 percent real. Our people get jobs because they know where journalism has to go, and they’re confident they can make it happen. Sound like the program for you? Read more...
